## 🌸 Who Am I?

Hey! I'm **Sama** — a Data Engineer based in **Cairo, Egypt** 🇪🇬

I graduated with a CS degree from **Helwan University** and completed the **ITI Intensive Data Engineering Track**, where I went deep on building real pipelines, warehouses, and end-to-end data systems.

I care about **clean architecture**, **reliable pipelines**, and turning raw, chaotic data into something that actually helps people make decisions.

Currently obsessed with: `Medallion Architecture` · `dbt transformations` · `Airflow orchestration`
